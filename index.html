Sidikalang Coffee

Sidikalang coffee is a term for robusta and arabica coffee which was developed in Sidikalang District, the capital city of Dairi Regency, North Sumatra. The Sidikalang coffee plantation area is located at an altitude of 1,500 meters above sea level, in the Bukit Barisan mountain area. Sidikalang coffee is considered as one of the best coffee varieties in Sumatra, along with Gayo coffee, Simalungun etc.
